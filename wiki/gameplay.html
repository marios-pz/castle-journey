Gameplay

Quests

Quests are the core progression system in Castle Journey. They guide you through the game's story and provide rewards for completion.

Quest States

  • Available: The quest is ready to be started
  • Started: The quest is in progress
  • Reached Goal: The quest objectives have been completed
  • Finished: The quest has been completed and rewards collected

Quest Panel

The quest panel displays:

  • Current quest title
  • Quest description and objectives
  • Progress updates

Inventory System

The inventory system allows you to manage your items and equipment.

Accessing Inventory

  • Press E or I to open/close the inventory
  • Items can be equipped, used, or discarded

Economy

The game's economy is based on gold, which is used for various purposes.

Gold System

  • Starting gold: 50
  • Earn gold by completing quests
  • Gold is displayed in the top-left corner of the screen
